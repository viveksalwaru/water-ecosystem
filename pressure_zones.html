AI escalation ladder

The controller works through actions in order — least invasive first. Trigger an event above to see which steps activate.

1
Adjust PRV setpoint
Raise or lower the pressure reducing valve to redistribute head across zones. Fast, non-disruptive.
2
Increase pump speed (VFD)
MPC raises VFD setpoint to boost system pressure. Uses more electricity but maintains supply to all zones.
3
Stage additional pump online
Start standby pump when a single pump at 100% cannot meet demand. Soft-start to avoid demand-charge spike.
4
Isolate affected zone valve
Close zone isolation valve to contain a burst or major leak, protecting network pressure in healthy zones.
5
Operator alert + field crew dispatch
SCADA alarm raised. AI pinpoints leak to within 2–3 pipe segments using pressure differential. Crew dispatched.
